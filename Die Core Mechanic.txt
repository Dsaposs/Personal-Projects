Die Core Mechanic

- Determine Target Number of Successes/Difficulty (default 0)
- Gather Dice Equal to the skill being checked
- Add extra die per advantage
- Remove extra die per disadvantage
- Roll
- Apply difficulty, remove one success per difficulty
	- if no dice remain check fails
- Determine Results
	- One 4 or higher results in success
	- No successes results in failure
	- if no successes and at least one 1 results in critical fail
- if success gain one Special per die with 6 over the target number
